1 About QSsh
2 ==========
3 
4 QSsh provides SSH and SFTP support for Qt applications. The aim of this project
5 is to provide a easy way to use these protocols in any Qt application.
6 
7 This project is based on Qt Creator's libQtcSsh.so. All the credits to
8 Qt Creator's team! (http://qt.gitorious.org/qt-creator)
9 
10 
11 ### Compiling QSsh
12 
13 Prerequisites:
14  * Qt 4.7.4 (May work with older versions)
15  * On Windows: MinGW 4.4 or later, Visual Studio 2008 or later
16  * On Mac: XCode 2.5 or later, compiling on 10.4 requires setting the
17  environment variable QTC_TIGER_COMPAT before running qmake
18 
19 Steps:
20 ```bash
21 mkdir $BUILD_DIRECTORY
22 cd $BUILD_DIRECTORY
23 qmake $SOURCE_DIRECTORY/qssh.pro
24 make (or mingw32-make or nmake depending on your platform)
25 ```
